Top 5 move-to-earn dapps

1. StepN

STEPN is the flagship move-to-earn platform. Originally built on Solana and later also on BNB Chain, it kickstarted the fitness movement on Web3.

Users need to set up an account, purchase a pair of StepN Sneakers NFTs (with SOL or BNB) and then start moving around in the real world. The more you move, the more Green Satoshi Tokens (GST) you earn.

In-game challenges like Marathon Mode keep players engaged and working towards a challenge. At the moment, though, there is a time-gated mechanic which caps players‚Äô earning potential at a certain point.

Check Out StepN

2. Genopets

Genopets began life back in August 2021 and is also one of the best move-to-earn dapps in 2023. Currently part of the BNB Chain ecosystem, the first-of-its-kind game combines NFT collectibles, battle arena gameplay, and move-to-earn functionality.

Players can level up their Genopet NFT avatars by doing exercises in the real world. The stronger your Genopet grows, the more likely they will be to conquer your opponents.

3. Step App

The Web3 fitness movement reached the Avalanche blockchain with the popular game Step App. Similar to StepN, users need to buy an NFT to get started earning from playing.

This game enters 2023 in style having as brand ambassador Olympic athlete Usain Bolt.

The platform‚Äôs utility token, KCAL, can be used to purchase it. As you exercise and register your performance using Step App, you are eligible for real-world rewards.
