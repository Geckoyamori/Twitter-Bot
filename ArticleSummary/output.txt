The move-to-earn movement
Like the play-to-earn movement, these games leverage blockchain technology to store users’ performance and reward them for time spent playing with cryptocurrency.

Despite being a rather new idea that seems to have come out of nowhere, many of the companies operating in the move-to-earn sector have been around for a few years already. Some clearly have big goals in mind as they look to connect the physical and the virtual worlds, one step at a time.
And as the industry evolves in 2023, so does the quality of these projects. As observed in the DappRadar x BGA Games Report of March 2023:
“Move-to-earn dapps remain a popular trend in the dapp world, despite some fluctuations in user activity. (…) With new features and developments on the horizon, these dapps are likely to continue growing and attracting new users in the coming months.”
Top 5 move-to-earn dapps
1. StepN
STEPN is the flagship move-to-earn platform. Originally built on Solana and later also on BNB Chain, it kickstarted the fitness movement on Web3.
Users need to set up an account, purchase a pair of StepN Sneakers NFTs (with SOL or BNB) and then start moving around in the real world. The more you move, the more Green Satoshi Tokens (GST) you earn.
In-game challenges like Marathon Mode keep players engaged and working towards a challenge. At the moment, though, there is a time-gated mechanic which caps players’ earning potential at a certain point.
2. Genopets
Genopets began life back in August 2021 and is also one of the best move-to-earn dapps in 2023. Currently part of the BNB Chain ecosystem, the first-of-its-kind game combines NFT collectibles, battle arena gameplay, and move-to-earn functionality.
Players can level up their Genopet NFT avatars by doing exercises in the real world. The stronger your Genopet grows, the more likely they will be to conquer your opponents. 
3. Step App
The Web3 fitness movement reached the Avalanche blockchain with the popular game Step App. Similar to StepN, users need to buy an NFT to get started earning from playing. 
This game enters 2023 in style having as brand ambassador Olympic athlete Usain Bolt.
The platform’s utility token, KCAL, can be used to purchase it. As you exercise and register your performance using Step App, you are eligible for real-world rewards.
4. Sweat Economy
Powered by the NEAR blockchain technology, Sweat Economy is another fitness dapp worth exploring this summer. It has already motivated over 120 thousand users to exercise – on Web2 and Web3.
It was first created in 2016 and only later added the crypto concept to it.
The project created its own cryptocurrency called SWEAT, that users can earn by walking and tracking their performance using the Sweat Economy app, Sweat Wallet. Learn more about this move-to-earn project in our complete guide.
5. The Dustland in OliveX
Part of the Polygon blockchain ecosystem, the Dustland is a move-to-earn game in OliveX’s fitness metaverse. Like others on our list, it rewards players in real life for exercising. This time, whether you’re running or cycling.
OliveX is a digital fitness company that uses gamification, augmented reality and play-to-earn gaming to deliver an entire ecosystem with the aim of improving people’s health. Not only is the platform building its own interactive virtual world within The Sandbox, it also has the Dustland series of games.
By going on runs and moving around in the real world, players get virtual rewards (NFTs/DOSE tokens) they can use to upgrade their avatar and progress on their adventure.
